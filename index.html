Convert your text now to upper case, lower case, capitalized case

The free online tool ConvertText is allow you to convert string into upper case, lower case and capitalized case. Then you can copy the converted text and paste it back to your document.

How it works?

Using the ConvertText tool is very simple. Choose the desired format and paste or typing your text in the text field. Your string is automatically convert in your chosen format. Then, copy the converted text by clicking the "Copy" button and paste it in your document.

In which cases can convert my text?

You can convert your text in 3 different cases:

Upper Case

All the letters from your text are converted in capital letters

Example: THIS IS A UPPER CASE TEXT

Lower Case

All the letters from your text are converted in small letters

Example: this is a lower case text

Capitalized Case

The first letter of every word from your text is converted in capital letter

Example: This Is A Capitalized Case Text
